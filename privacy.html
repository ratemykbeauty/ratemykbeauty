Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Welcome to ratemykbeauty.com. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a review, we collect:

  • Your name or username
  • Your review text, star rating, and skin type
  • The product name, brand, and retailer
  • Receipt image (to verify your purchase)
  • Product photo (optional, if you choose to upload one)
  • A browser fingerprint (to prevent duplicate likes)

We do not collect your email address, phone number, or any payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your review publicly on ratemykbeauty.com
  • To verify that reviews come from real buyers
  • To prevent spam and fake reviews
  • To improve our service and user experience

3. Receipt Images

Receipt images are uploaded solely to verify that you purchased the product you are reviewing. They are stored securely in Supabase Storage and are not shared with third parties. We recommend blurring or cropping any sensitive personal information (such as your full name or address) before uploading.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Data is hosted in the United States. We retain your review data indefinitely unless you request deletion.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This may involve cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We also store a browser fingerprint in your local storage to prevent duplicate likes. This does not identify you personally.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and file storage
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your review or any data associated with your submission. To do so, email us at ratemykbeauty@gmail.com with the details of your review.

8. Children's Privacy

ratemykbeauty.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at ratemykbeauty@gmail.com.
